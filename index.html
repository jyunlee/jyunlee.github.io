CV | Google Scholar | GitHub

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Vision and Learning Lab at KAIST advised by Prof. Tae-Kyun (T-K) Kim. I am also co-advised by Prof. Minhyuk Sung. My current research interests lie in machine learning for 3D computer vision to model human bodies and hands.

Before joining KAIST, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Handong Global University with Summa Cum Laude and early graduation. I also have a memorable experience of attending an American international high school in Nepal, where I learned the value of cross-cultural skills and teamwork. Besides research, I am also interested in music composition and arrangement.
